FAQ

Frequently Asked Question

Why AgileAsia

Our instructors are practitioners and thought leaders in their respective areas with years of applied industry experience. Balanced with a deep understanding of instructional design and pedagogy, our world-class trainers and instructors are able to help your organisation and people maximise the learning outcomes of each course.

Who can join the courses in AgileAsia?

At AgileAsia, all are welcome! There are however, different levels of courses varying from Business to Tech, hence the prerequisites differ for each course.

Is there funding available for courses at AgileAsia?

Courses funded under IBF-STS Certified Scrum Master (CSM) Courses funded by CITREP+ & SkillsFuture: Certified Scrum Product Owner (CSPO) Agile Software Engineering ICAgile DevOps Foundations User Experience Design Foundations Design Sprint & Innovation Robotic Process Automation for Business (RPA) Other courses provided by AgileAsia might be supported by other funding programmes as well. Check in with us!

Am I eligible for 100% subsidy?

Students and/or Full Time National Service (NSF) (Singapore Citizen) Up to 100% of the nett payable course and certification fees, capped at $2,500 per trainee. Professionals (Singapore Citizen and Permanent Resident) Up to 70% of the nett payable course and certification fees capped at S$3,000 per trainee. Professionals 40 years old and above (Singapore Citizen) Up to 90% of the nett payable course and certification fees capped at S$3,000 per trainee. Professionals 40 years old and above (Permanent Resident) Up to 70% of the nett payable course and certification fees capped at S$3,000 per trainee. Please refer to the page for more details : https://www.imda.gov.sg/imtalent/programmes/citrep-plus

How do I know if I am eligible for funding, and how does it work?

All Singapore Citizens and Singaporean PRs are eligible for up to 100% funding! You can check on your eligibility and all the grant information on the official website here – https://www.imda.gov.sg/imtalent/programmes/citrep-plus. You can also drop us a message at 93852234 or training@agileasia.com. We are happy to help!

My organisation is sponsoring me to join the course. How do I do that?

For organisation-sponsored individuals, registrations are done as per normal. Once your seat in the course has been confirmed, a AgileAsia representative will be in contact with you to assist you.

What will I get at the end of the course?

Certified Scrum Product Owner® (CSPO) course – You will be receiving a CSPO Certification by Scrum Alliance upon successful completion of the course. Certified Scrum Master ® (CSM) course – You will be receiving a CSM Certification by Scrum Alliance upon successful completion of the course. ICAgile DevOps Foundations course – You will be receiving a ICP-FDO by ICAgile upon successful completion of the course. Upon completion of all the other courses, you will receive a Certification of Attendance from AgileAsia. Apart from that, we will also provide post-course advisory services for all our trainees!

What is the class size?

Our class sizes vary from 10-25 students

Will food and refreshments be provided?

Yes, lunch and two coffee breaks with snacks will be provided for a full day course.

What if I cannot make it for one (or more) of the lessons?

Classes are fixed and there will not be make-up classes available.

What are the payment options available?

Enrolment will be facilitated through Eventbrite which allows for payment using a debit/credit card as well as PayPal. Online bank tranfer facility is available. Offline payment cheque, draft and cashier order etc will be accepted Acknowledgement and receipt of payment will be issued by email once payment is completed.

I am not a Singapore Citizen or PR, do you offer discounts?

Yes, we offer a variety of discounts depending on the number of people registering for the course. Check in with us at training@agileasia.com

I would like to arrange for private/corporate training, what is the process?

You can contact us at training@agileasia.com and we can make the arrangements.

Is there any refund after registration?

You can contact us at training@agileasia.com and we can make the arrangements.

How to claim PDU’s with PMI

How to claim PDU’s for CSM and CSPO Login to your PMI account Select -> Report PDUs Select -> Course or Training Option For Provider -> Type AgileAsia (Do not select an option from the dropdown For Course -> Type Certified ScrumMaster or Certified Scrum Product Owner (Do not select an option from the dropdown Enter the start and end dates of the class For URL -> enter agileasia.com For contact email -> enter training@agileasia.com For PDUs claimed -> split the hours evenly between Technical [8.00] , Leadership [4.00] and Strategic [4.00] Agree and submit
